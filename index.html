Welcome to Sweetest Drop Market!

Times are hard, and as such we made sure you get through this safe and sound!

To ensure this happens, we offer one complementary spray, to keep those invading your personal space at bay!

To make use of it, press [Space] whenever someone gets too close for confort, but be careful, you have a limited amount!

Pick what you need by pressing [E], and procceed to one of the available checkouts (yellow rectangles).

Checkouts become available when you pick the first 5 items, but you can pick up more to increase your score!

Be careful however, for each 5 items you pick up, the clients will become faster!

In case you forgot how to walk, you can do this by pressing [WASD].

We at Sweetest Drop Market thank you for your preference and more importantly for your money, please come back soon!
